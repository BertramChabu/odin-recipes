StrawBerry Bellini

A strawberry bellini is a nice drink for Mother's Day, a brunch, or a shower.

Ingredients

  • 3 cup strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • ¼ cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1 tablespoon brandy
  • 1 ½ cups chilled sparkling wine
  • 3 large whole strawberries

Procedures

  • blend strawberries:

    strawberries, and chilled sparkling wine in a blender until smooth. Chill in the refrigerator for 10 minutes.

  • pour strawberry bellini:

    strawberry bellini into a cocktail glass. Top with ice.

  • serve strawberry bellini:

    strawberry bellini into a cocktail glass. Top with ice.
